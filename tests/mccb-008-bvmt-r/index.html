Brief Visuospatial Memory Test-Revised (BVMT-R)

Test Instructions

This test measures your visual memory. You will see 6 geometric figures arranged in a 2Ã—3 grid.

Procedure:

  • You will see the figures for 10 seconds
  • Then you'll draw as many as you can remember in the correct positions
  • This will be repeated for 3 learning trials
  • After a 25-minute delay, you'll be asked to draw them again
  • Finally, you'll identify the original figures from a set of 12

Try to remember both the shapes and their positions!

Time: 10

Learning Trial 1

Study these 6 figures carefully. You have 10 seconds to memorize them and their positions.
